Cookie Policy
At AVD Company, we use cookies to provide better service to our website visitors, enhance their experiences, and deliver personalized content. This policy explains what cookies are, what types of cookies we use, and how cookies can be controlled.
What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files sent to your browser when you visit a website and stored on your computer or mobile device. Cookies are used for various purposes, such as remembering user preferences, enabling more efficient use of the site, and analyzing site traffic.
Types of Cookies We Use:
1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website and are essential for basic functions like navigating the site and using its features. Without these cookies, requested services cannot be provided.
2. Performance Cookies: These cookies gather information about how visitors use our website. We use this information to improve our website and enhance user experience. Performance cookies collect anonymous information and do not identify visitors.
3. Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember choices you make when visiting our website (e.g., your username, language preferences, or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.
4. Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver content and ads that are more relevant to your interests. They also help us measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
Controlling Cookies: You can choose to accept or decline cookies by changing your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect some functions of our website. For more information on how to manage cookies through your browser settings, you can visit your browser's help menu.
Privacy and Security: Information collected through cookies is stored and processed in accordance with our privacy policy. The security of your personal data is important to us, and we take all necessary measures regarding the use of cookies.
For more information about our cookie policy, please contact us.
